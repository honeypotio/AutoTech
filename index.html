The Wheel
of AutoTech

Honeypot started this project by analysing six major German automakers and automotive supplier companies: Volkswagen, Daimler, BMW, Schaeffler, Robert Bosch and Sixt. We then mapped the relationships to the emerging AutoTech players and related partners. The intention of this wheel is to provide software developers with an overview of the opportunities in this exciting industry. We define AutoTech as the application of software to carmaking in the pursuit of automonous driving.
